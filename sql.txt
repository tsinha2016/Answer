Select  CUSTOMER.ID,CUSTOMER.NAME from CUSTOMER ordered by CUSTOMER.NAME desc
Select  STUDENT.ID,STUDENT.NAME,STUDENT.MARKS,
CASE 
WHEN STUDENT.MARKS BETWEEN 90 AND 100   THEN ‘A+’
WHEN STUDENT.MARKS BETWEEN 70 AND 90  THEN ‘A’
WHEN STUDENT.MARKS BETWEEN 50 AND 70    THEN ‘B’
WHEN STUDENT.MARKS BETWEEN 40 AND 50 THEN ‘C’
WHEN STUDENT.MARKS<40  THEN ‘Fail’
END AS GRADE
FROM  STUDENT
